Sparrho launched their Early Career Researcher Prize initiative in June 2017 to offer grants of £500 for PhD students and postdocs in any field of science, at any institute, to attend an academic conference of their choice in the coming 12 months. Each winner of the Sparrho Early Career Researcher Prize will also be featured in the Huffington Post.

Founded in 2013 by two Oxbridge scientists, Sparrho is a global scientific discovery platform that combines artificial intelligence and human expert curation to help researchers from 150+ countries stay on top of the science that matters to them.
